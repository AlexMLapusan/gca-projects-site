Services

What we build

Six trades, one crew, from the first shovel to the final brush.

Terraces & paving

Porcelain tile, clinker brick and natural stone, laid laser-level on a proper foundation, with drainage that takes Dutch rain seriously.

Pergolas & verandas

Douglas fir structures, cut and fitted on site: a frame for your terrace, a canopy over your head.

Natural & artificial lawns

Fresh sod or premium turf on a levelled base. Flat, green and ready for bare feet.

Planting & raised beds

Raised planters in brick and stacked stone, olive trees, grasses and borders that finish the picture.

Fencing & privacy

Timber and composite fences: straight, solid and built to hold their line in every season.

Maintenance & pruning

Hedges, shrubs and trees pruned or removed. The heavy, messy work, cleared away by evening.

How we work

Four steps, no surprises

01

Visit & plan

We come by, measure, and listen to how you want to use the garden. You get a clear plan and a sharp quote, with no surprises later.

02

Groundwork & drainage

The part you never see decides how long a garden lasts: excavation, a stable sub-base, drainage sized for Dutch rain.

03

Build & finish

Tiles laid laser-level with levelling clips, timber cut on site, edges closed clean. Hard work, precise hands.

04

Handover & aftercare

We brush, wash and walk you through the result. If something isn't right, we come back.

About

A small crew that shows up

GCA Projects is a registered Dutch gardening company from Bergen op Zoom, founded by craftsmen who do the work themselves, from the first shovel to the final brush. The people who quote your garden are the people who build it.

We work across West-Brabant and Zeeland: Bergen op Zoom, Halsteren, Hoogerheide, Oosterhout and around. You can reach us in English, Dutch or Romanian.
